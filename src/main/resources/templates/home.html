Welcome to SAML2 Client Demo

This is a demonstration of SAML2 authentication with CAS server.

Available Actions:

  • Login with SAML2
  • Go to Dashboard (requires authentication)

SAML2 Endpoints:

  • SP Metadata: /saml2/service-provider-metadata/cas
  • SSO Endpoint: /login/saml2/sso/cas
  • Logout Endpoint: /logout
